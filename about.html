My Approach

Every project starts with understanding the *why* before writing a single line of code. Here's how I operate.

01

Discovery & Research

Deep-dive into the problem space. Interview stakeholders, audit existing systems, map user flows, and identify the core constraints before committing to any architecture.

02

Prototype & Validate

Build fast, throw away what doesn't work. I ship throwaway prototypes to test critical assumptions before investing in production-grade code. Fail fast, learn faster.

03

Build & Iterate

Incremental delivery with tight feedback loops. Every sprint ships something usable. CI/CD from day one. Automated testing. Observability baked in, not bolted on.

04

Refine & Elevate

The last 10% is where the magic lives. Performance tuning, accessibility audits, micro-interactions, polished edge cases — the details that separate good from exceptional.
